Welcome, Colonel!

Enemy troops were seen on our borders. We need your help to organize our army and destroy them!

    What to do:

  1. You will be given same number of troops as our enemy.
  2. Deploy our troops across the battlefields in non-decrescent order.
  3. You win the battle by conquering more battlefields than the enemy.
  4. Advance to next battles as you gain more experience.
